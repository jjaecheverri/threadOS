— 02

The Problem

Drift is invisible
until it isn't.

AI workflows have a continuity problem. Across model switches, long sessions, multi-step processes, and collaborative handoffs, the original intent — the clarity of the first idea — erodes quietly and continuously.

By the time drift becomes visible, the damage is done. Output is off. Rework is needed. The thread is lost. Drifty catches drift before it reaches that point.

Stage 01 — Clear Intent

Strong first prompt. Coherent starting point. Direction is intact.

Stage 02 — Expansion Begins

Context grows. Model switches. Subtle slippage starts accumulating.

Stage 03 — Drift Compounds

Output diverges from original intent. The gap widens invisibly.

Without Drifty

Thread is lost. Rework required. Quality cost realized too late.

With Drifty

Drift caught. Thread held. Output integrity preserved from the start.

— 04

Naming Logic

Why Drifty is the right name for this product.

DRIFTY
drift control continuity layer coherence infrastructure result integrity system signal-preservation tool
01

Semantically loaded. "Drifty" owns the concept it solves. The name is the problem space. That's rare — and powerful for brand clarity and recall.

02

Short and ownable. One syllable root, five letters total. Registers fast, sticks, and survives every medium — domain, app store, pitch deck, word of mouth.

03

Edgy through restraint. The "-y" suffix adds texture without softness. It reads as current and culturally intelligent without leaning into techno-jargon or generic noun naming.

04

Built for a system. Drifty, Drifty Studio, Drifty Flow, Drifty Watch — the root extends cleanly into a product family without strain or explanation.

05

Carries both truths. Names the problem (drift) and implies the solution (something that catches it). The name alone communicates the full product logic.

Logo & Mark Concepts

Five conceptual directions. Not final artwork — smart starting points for design development.

01

Ring + Thread

A clean ring with thread lines and centered catch node. Precision and balance in one mark.

02

Tension Monogram

The letter D formed by a tensioned arc anchored to a spine. The structure itself is the meaning.

03

Mesh Capture

A geometric web structure — outer and inner frames converging on a central catch node. Diagrammatic, architectural.

04

Minimal Orbital

Concentric orbital rings with a tethered catch point. Highly scalable, strong at icon size.

05

Signal Net

A diamond form with layered internal structure and catch nodes. Angular, precise, architecturally solid.

Direction Recommendation Concepts 01 and 04 are the strongest starting points for commercial development. Both scale cleanly to icon size, hold strong in black-and-white applications, and carry the catch/continuity logic without requiring explanation. Concept 02 (Tension Monogram) is the highest-risk, highest-reward direction — best explored as a secondary mark or wordmark treatment once the primary system is locked.
